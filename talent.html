Our Commitment to Candidate Feedback

We believe feedback is an important part of a respectful recruiting process. Clear communication helps candidates gain insight and direction, regardless of interview outcomes.

When feedback is provided by clients, we communicate it professionally and constructively, focusing on:

  • • Role-specific skills and experience
  • • Interview strengths and development areas
  • • Alignment with job requirements

Some decisions remain confidential to employers, but we aim to share meaningful insights whenever possible.
