Privacy Policy

Bavix Ally Group, LLC respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information collected through our website and recruiting services.

Information We Collect

Information is collected solely to provide staffing and recruiting services, evaluate candidates, communicate with users, and comply with legal obligations.

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for staffing services, legal compliance, or dispute resolution, after which it is securely deleted.
